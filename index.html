About Us

Where dedication meets the soil

Our Story

Faithworks Venture Farming is a growing agricultural enterprise rooted in the fertile lands of St Faith's, Rusape Osborne area. Spanning 10 hectares of prime farming land, we are committed to producing high-quality agricultural products while promoting sustainable farming practices.

From our humble beginnings with Kalahari Red goats and maize, we have grown into a diversified farming operation with a vision to become a leading agricultural producer in the Manicaland region.

Our Ventures

Diversified agriculture for a thriving future

Active

Kalahari Red Goat Farming

We raise premium Kalahari Red goats, renowned for their excellent meat quality, fast growth rate, and adaptability to Zimbabwe's climate. Our breeding program focuses on genetic improvement and herd health.

Active

Maize Cropping

Large-scale maize production utilizing modern farming techniques. We grow high-yield varieties suited to the Rusape climate, ensuring consistent supply for local markets and bulk buyers.

Active

Covo & Rape Fields

Fresh, nutritious vegetable production supplying local communities, schools, and markets. Our covo and rape fields are cultivated with organic practices for healthy, chemical-free greens.

Coming Soon

Seedling Production

We plan to establish a seedling nursery to supply farmers with quality vegetable and crop seedlings, supporting agricultural development across the region.

Cattle Fattening

Expansion into beef cattle fattening to meet growing demand for quality meat. We will focus on feedlot management and pasture-based finishing.

Roadrunner Chickens
